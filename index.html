FAQ
Common Questions

Everything Lake County homeowners ask before booking.

How much does pressure washing cost in Lake County? +

Pressure washing in Lake County typically runs $199–$649 depending on what surfaces need cleaning and your home size. A standard 1-story house exterior starts at $199. Driveways start at $109. Bundle 3+ surfaces for 10% off. Use the calculator above to see your exact price instantly.

How much does junk removal cost in Lake County? +

Junk removal ranges from $99 for a minimum load to $699+ for a full truckload. A quarter truck (a few pieces of furniture) is typically $150–$175. A half truck (small room cleanout) runs $250–$350. Price is confirmed on-site — if your load is smaller than estimated, you pay less. You pay only after the job is done.

Do I need to be home during the service? +

No. Most exterior services — pressure washing, gutter cleaning, window cleaning, pet waste removal — don't require you to be home. Just ensure gate access if applicable. We'll text you when we arrive and again when we're done with photos.

When is the best time to get gutters cleaned in Lake County? +

The best window is September–November, after most leaves have fallen and before the Illinois ground freezes. Cleaning before the first hard freeze prevents ice dams and water backup damage. Spring (April–May) is the second best time after winter debris accumulation.

Are your technicians licensed and insured? +

Yes. Every technician we dispatch carries general liability insurance naming Partna Company LLC as additionally insured. We require a Certificate of Insurance (COI) on file before any crew is dispatched to your home. You are fully protected.

How does pet waste removal subscription work? +

Month-to-month starting at $95/month for weekly visits (1 dog, standard yard). No contracts — cancel anytime. The first visit includes a full catch-up clean at no extra charge. We serve all Lake County IL zip codes year-round, including winter service.

Do you serve all of Lake County IL? +

Yes. We cover the entire county — Grayslake, Lake Forest, Libertyville, Vernon Hills, Mundelein, Lake Zurich, Wauconda, Gurnee, Highland Park, Round Lake Beach, Waukegan, Antioch, Fox Lake, Zion, North Chicago and all surrounding communities.
